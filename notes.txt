To pass arrays from HTML to PHP:
1. Use serialize and unserialize
Client:  '<input type="hidden" name="items" value=' .serialize($items). '/>'
Server:  $items = unserialize($_POST['items']);

2. User implode and explode
Client:  '<input type="hidden" name="items" value="' .implode(",", $items). '"/>'
Server:  $items = explode(",", $_POST['items']);
